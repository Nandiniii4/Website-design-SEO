Top Picks from My Bookshelf

Here’s a peek at some of the stories I swear by, from spells to secrets and everything in between.

🧙🪄Harry Potter Series

by J.K. Rowling

The magical world that started it all — spells, friendship, and a lightning scar. Full confession: haven’t finished books 5-8 yet, but the journey’s been epic so far!

🐾👻Natsume’s Book of Friends (Manga)

by Yuki Midorikawa

Heartwarming and mysterious, this manga follows a kind soul who can see spirits. It’s gentle, emotional, and full of beautiful moments.

⚔️🖤Shadowhunter Series

by Cassandra Clare

Angel blades, demons, and dark secrets — a YA urban fantasy that keeps you on your toes with every twist.

🧙🪄Enid Blyton’s Mystery Series

by Enid Blyton

Childhood nostalgia in a book! Classic whodunits, secret passages, and unforgettable adventures that hooked me as a kid.

🦅🃏Six of Crows

by Leigh Bardugo

A crew of misfits, heists, and magic—this dark fantasy heist story is as thrilling as it is heart-wrenching.
